Privacy Policy for Hailspeaker

Last updated: 29-April-2024

Introduction

Welcome to Hailspeaker! As a virtual megaphone app, Hailspeaker allows you to connect a Bluetooth speaker to your iOS device and project your voice. Simultaneously, your speech is transcribed within the app, which you can view and export as needed.

Information Collection and Use

Hailspeaker does not collect any personal information from its users. The app functions independently without storing, processing, or transmitting personal data to any servers or third-party services.

Transcriptions

The transcriptions generated by the app are stored locally on your device. Hailspeaker does not access, store, or share these transcriptions with any third parties. Users have the option to export their transcriptions at their discretion.
