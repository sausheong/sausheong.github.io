Welcome to qaPDF Support

qaPDF is an innovative iOS app that allows you to not only view PDF documents but also engage in conversations with them. Have a question about the content of your PDF? Just ask! qaPDF utilizes advanced AI to analyze your document and provide accurate, context-aware answers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to commonly asked questions:

  • Q: How do I ask a document a question?
    A: Simply open a PDF document in qaPDF and tap the "Chat" button. Type your question in the input field and hit send.
  • Q: What types of questions can I ask?
    A: You can ask anything related to the content of the PDF. For example, you could ask for definitions, summaries of specific sections, or comparisons between different parts of the document.
  • Q: Is my data secure?
    A: qaPDF processes your documents locally on your device and does not store them on external servers.
  • Q: Which AI provider do you use?
    A: qaPDF uses Google Gemini-1.5-Flash with 1 million token context size. You can create your API key here -- https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey. Note that Gemini-1.5-Flash is free with rate limits and conditions. In particular, Google is allowed to use your prompts and responses to train its models. To avoid this, you can create a paid account by setting up billing. Do not submit sensitive, confidential, or personal information to Google's unpaid services.
